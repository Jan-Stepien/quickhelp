## Reddit posts (one per target subreddit from SPEC.md)

For each subreddit:

**Title rules:**
- Lead with the problem or outcome, never the product name
- No "I built" in the title (that's for Show HN)
- No exclamation marks
- Under 100 characters

**Body rules:**
- First paragraph: the problem you/others faced (personal, specific)
- Second paragraph: what the tool does in one sentence + link
- End with: "It's free, no login required. Feedback welcome."
- Never mention pricing in the first post

## Hacker News

Format: `Show HN: [App Name] – [one-line description]`
Body: 2-3 sentences. What it does, why you built it, the tech (Next.js + Claude API). Link.

## Twitter/X thread

Tweet 1: The hook — a surprising number or painful truth about the problem
Tweet 2: What you built + link (the payoff)
Tweet 3: What's next / what you want feedback on

## Product Hunt (if launching there)

Tagline: Under 60 characters. Outcome-first.
Description: 3 bullets — problem, solution, one surprising use case.
First comment: your personal story of why you built it.
